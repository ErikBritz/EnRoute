What This Site Does

How It Works

  • Upload a Movebank tracking CSV
  • Species name matches to AVONET traits
  • The system computes cost of transport
  • Visualization after data is matched

Key Variables

  • Track: Latitude, Longitude, Timestamp, Ground Speed.
  • Traits: Mass (g), Wing Length (mm), Hand-Wing Index.
  • Derived: Wing Span (m), Aspect Ratio, Wing Area (m²).

Core Formulae

AR = 2.04 ln(HWI) + 2.58
S = b² / AR
COTd = Ptotal / V

Full aerodynamic terms are applied in the backend calculation.
